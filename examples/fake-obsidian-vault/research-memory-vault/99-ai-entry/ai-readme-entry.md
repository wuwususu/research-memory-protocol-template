## Baseline Requirements

- Do not fabricate data, statistical results, literature, DOI, PMID, patient information, or experimental results.
- Do not present hypotheses as facts.
- Do not describe association as causation.
- AI output is `Unverified until checked` by default.
- Write back a Task Summary at the end of the task, but final conclusions require user confirmation.
