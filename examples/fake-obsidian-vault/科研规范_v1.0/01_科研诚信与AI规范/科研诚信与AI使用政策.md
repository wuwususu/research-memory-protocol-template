# 科研诚信与 AI 使用政策

## Core Rule

AI can organize, summarize, draft, and check logic. AI cannot invent data, literature, statistics, or medical conclusions.

## Evidence Status

All AI outputs are `Unverified until checked`.

## Prohibited

- Fabricated sample sizes
- Fabricated P values or confidence intervals
- Fabricated DOI / PMID / titles
- Synthetic data used as research evidence
- Correlation written as causality
- Hypothesis written as confirmed mechanism
